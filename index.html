Our Mission

We, the student body of the Luddy School of Informatics, Computing, and Engineering (Luddy) at Indiana University, establish this Constitution of the Luddy Student Government (LSG) on June 12th, 2025, to:

  1. Serve as the voice of the Luddy student body
  2. Collaborate with faculty and administration to influence Luddy School of Informatics, Computing, and Engineering decisions, incentives, and policies
  3. Facilitate a strong relationship between students and faculty
  4. Organize collaborative social and educational programs for present, former, and future Luddy students
  5. Facilitate collaboration and information sharing among member organizations
  6. Encourage community throughout the Luddy School of Informatics, Computing, and Engineering
  7. Responsibly distribute funds allocated by the Luddy Office of Student Engagement and Success to approved Luddy University sponsored organizations
  8. Promote the Luddy School of Informatics, Computing, and Engineering in the local community and among businesses around the United States
  9. Improve the overall experience of students throughout their time at Luddy

To that end, we hereby establish the Luddy Student Government in accordance with the criteria set forth herein.
